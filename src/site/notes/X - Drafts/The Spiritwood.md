## Overview
--- 
>A large, dense forest, submerged in memoria. Blanketed in darkness broken up by streams of mysterious blue light, it is a place of mystery and curiosity for all. 
### Description :
It is always night time here, something about the canopy above encases the undergrowth below. Light is primarily provided by the presence of swarms of flying creatures above and faint yellow flowers growing from the trees. Some trees grow in clusters, resembling willow trees, these trees provide large amounts of light as the flowers will grow in their leaves in cascades. The ground is covered with a mix of mist, roots, and grass. If one looks closely, they may notice that the grass here is simply an extension of the trees, similar to a fungal growth. Creeks cut through the underbrush at times, flowing from a much, much larger source unseen to the eye. Apparitions are also a frequent sight here, with pale or dark forms lurking at the corner of one’s vision. Visitors are often struck by a sense of being underwater, submerged in something intangible to sight. 
### Background:
- It is well-known that the Spiritwood is a place that lies on the border between the mundane and mystical. Apparitions and ghostly figures lie at the periphery of the vision of travelers, and hallucinations of the past are common here.
	- As a result of this, disappearances are common. 
- It is commonly believed that the cause is a mysterious figure that few have witnessed and lived to tell the tale of. The Hunter. Their shadowy figure is commonly seen by travelers shortly before tragedy strikes. 
	- The Hunter is not, in fact, the cause of these happenings, as many would believe, but merely an unwilling witness to events that they are often too late to stop. 
- The Spiritwood is aptly named, not for its mystical properties, but for the fact that the forest is well and truly alive. 
	- It sustains its growth through consuming travelers & and extracting their memoria. The hallucinations and apparitions that plague travelers are plucked from the memories of those who have fallen, luring them deeper and deeper into the wood. 
	- At the center of the wood lies a crumbling ruin, and in it's center a small sapling, whose true form is a massive jellyfish. 
	    - This entity is the quarry of the Hunter, who has been locked in an endless quest to free the forest of its curse. 
- Should the Hunter ever succeed in their quest to slay the beast that plagues the wood, the tide of memoria would surge and submerge the forest, blanketing it and revealing the true memories that have been locked within. 
     - The spirit of the wood is trying to maintain the wood as best it can, attempting to preserve the things it collects.
