## Overview
--- 
### Heroes:
- Heroes are those who fight for an ideal. This ideal may be mundane, extraordinary, or anything in between, but the individual must devote themselves to its pursuit. 

### Qualities:
- Heroes may pursue vastly different ideals, but the pace and perspective they approach their journey with generally falls into recognizable patterns. 
- Generally, they are listed as follows:
	- **Bravery (David)**
		- Perhaps one of the first traits one might consider when imagining the vision of a hero. It is a willingness to stand against doubt and uncertainty, and to take action in the face of uncertainty. 
		- **Gameplay: Associated with high-risk gambles and performance checks, rewards skill and risky play.**
		- (?) Delusion: Makes Performance Checks more punishing on failure.
	- **Ferocity (Achilles)**
		- The intensity of a hero's drive is what propels their actions to the status of legend. It is in how one asserts their will upon the world that changes the status quo. 
		- **Gameplay: Associated with powerful attacks with big sacrifices, rewards aggressive play.**
	- **Tenacity (Joan of Arc)**
		- Unwavering persistence. No matter the struggle, a hero perseveres.
		- **Gameplay:** **Associated with sustain and weaker initial damage, rewards patient play.**
	- **Alacrity (Aladdin)**
		- The ability to seek out opportunity, and grasp the hand of fortune with a keen eye is a skill none can live without. 
		- **Gameplay: Associated with action economy, rewards .**
	- **Trickery (Odysseus)**
		- Cunning, sleight of hand, and unconventional means can give even the powerless the ability to make their mark on history. 
		- **Gameplay: Associated with DoT, Debuffs, & Statuses.**
	- **Integrity (Superman)**
		- Honesty, truthfulness, and being of pure intention can clear away much in the way of uncertainty. 
		- **Gameplay: Associated with clearing statuses, and delusion economy.**
