## Description:
A stubby sprout flora often found in forested areas, [[X - Misc/XXX/Germi\|Germi]] are creatures of folly. Upon sprouting, each [[X - Misc/XXX/Germi\|Germi]] will be imprinted with a dream at random, which they will pursue for the rest of their lives. Unfortunately, they are cursed with an intelligence ill-fit for their lofty ambitions, and many do not survive to mature into a fully-fledged sprout. Speculation on what a fully-sprouted [[X - Misc/XXX/Germi\|Germi]] may look like is hotly debated, as sightings are few and far in between. Those who say they have seen a mature [[Germi \|Germi ]] in the wild often describe ~~wretched creatures consumed by a ceaseless quest~~ hallucinations of departed loved ones.
