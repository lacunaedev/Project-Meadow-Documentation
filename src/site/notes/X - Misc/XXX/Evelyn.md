	- Lightly armored.
### Personality & Demeanor
- Quixotic, wistful, steadfast, kind-hearted, but impulsive, perfectionist, & short-sighted.
- Is consumed by a burning desire to be **somebody, anybody special.** 
	- Punishing in her own self-critique, she often takes on black & white perspectives of the situations she finds herself in, and often gets in her own way when attempting to make good on her promises to the people around her, causing immense internal anguish and distress. 
	- Takes on a grandiose, larger than life persona to make her feel more like the hero she ought to be.
- She fears being rejected more than anything else, especially when her ability to perform as a hero is called into question. Her combat prowess is everything to her, and she is incredibly prideful of the style that she has cultivated.
- She takes on people's problems left and right as a result, and often attempts to impose her own objectives to go above and beyond the letter of the quests she embarks on. This leads to frustration & angst when she cannot fulfill them all or if she fails an objective and doesn't communicate the issues back. 
- However, underneath the awkwardness & delusions of grandeur is a warm soul that wants nothing more than to bring joy to the world, and connect with others through it.
