## Description:
This mysterious creature takes the form of a large, majestic moon jellyfish. It undulates and twists slowly in the air, propelled by some unseen force, its long tentacles trailing gently behind, tangling themselves in the forest around it. Within its bulbous cap, witnesses have reported seeing flashes of spaces they do not recognize and faces of strangers they have never met. 

Algnosta act as detritovores, absorbing ambient memories through their tentacles and storing them within their core. This grants them the unique ability to manifest & play back events they have consumed as a means of protection. Algnosta are known to display sentience to a degree, and form deep attachments to the memories they ingest. On occasion, consuming memoria attached to powerful emotions may cause them to mutate and take on the characteristics of those associated with them.

Their life cycle is similar to mundane jellyfish. Large masses of lost memoria coalesce into a polyp, which floats to a space and begins to slowly absorb it from the air, in this state. This continues until they come into contact with strong memories and metamorphize, taking on a medusa form and stabilizing the space to play them back. Should they be significantly harmed, they will quickly revert back to a polyp, and expel all but one of their accumulated memories, continuing the cycle with this unique identity. Memories of longing and unfulfillment often form the core of this identity. 

These creatures usually inhabit [[The Deep Blue\|The Deep Blue]], and are partially responsible for it's abyssal form, but can be found wherever there is a high saturation of lost memoria. The one caught in the center of the Spiritwood is one such case, having taken on the dream of a young girl, longing for the carefree days of her youth to continue forever...
