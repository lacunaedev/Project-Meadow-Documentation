### Quest Draft 0:
Vel is given a quest to hunt a mysterious entity in the woods that the people simply call “The Reaper”. Supposedly, it has been seen stalking people who travel through the woods, many of whom disappear without a trace. Vel explores the forest, hunting for this spirit, catching glimpses of it as she draws near, until it is spooked, and attacks her. She battles it fiercely, and whittles down its fearsome aura until all that is left is… a childlike spirit. She is stricken, and as her resolve falters, the world goes dark,  and a fearsome persona takes her over, tearing the screen in two…

## Quest Details
## Logline
*An eager hero on a grand adventure investigates a mysterious forest haunted by the tide of an illusory sea, and whispers of a phantom lurking within it.*
## Description
> During her travels, Vel finds her path blocked by the impassable Spiritwood, a massive forest that spans across much of the surrounding regions. A place known to summon apparitions of the past before those who find themselves lost within, it has amassed a dangerous reputation for travelers, with recent sightings of oceanic creatures and a silent phantom that follows. 
> 
> After getting lost and wandering around the forest, she finds herself in Nature's Rest, a small village located just outside the forest line. There, she hears of the plight of the forest, and the fears of the townsfolk, who direct Vel to follow the sigils left behind by the phantom. 
> 
> Venturing into the wood with newfound purpose, she encounters [[X - Drafts/Myo, The Hunter\|Myo]], the hunter known as the mysterious phantom of the woods. They tell her of the true threat that besieges the forest, a fearsome beast that lures travelers deeper into the dense brush where they are never seen again. They tell her that the sigils left behind by them are to deter travelers from falling prey to its tentacles. 
> 
> Vel agrees to assist Myo with their hunt, and they continue to delve deeper and deeper into the wood, where the light is tinted with a deep blue hue, and all life sways not to the breeze, but to an invisible current. They learn that Myo has been searching for the beast in an effort to find a loved one, someone who's been missing for some time, and that they hope to bring them back home to the village. This coincides around the time that the strange ocean around them has started to manifest. Myo is determined to get to the bottom of things, and return to the days of peace they enjoyed as a child. 
> 
> Oddly enough, Vel cannot find anyone in town who mentions any missing persons.
> 
> They delve deeper, and find [[X - Drafts/Algnosta\|Algnosta]], a massive jellyfish-like creature at the center of the ethereal ocean encroaching on the wood. After a struggle, Algnosta exposes Vel & Myo to a vision, in which it is revealed that it consumes memories, and manifested as the wishes of a girl who wished for a friend. (More detailed lore in Obsidian, long story short is that Algnosta is a memetic creature, and took on the visage of the girl and befriended Myo all those years ago. The village does not remember Myo because it isn't real, just a figment of their memories, made real.)

## DEMO VERSION
> Upon learning this, Myo is shaken, and hesitates to deal the final blow, as does Vel, strugging to reconcile what she should do as a hero. However, as they pause, something rises up within Vel, and a shadow rises up to guide her sword into the beast, after which a flash of light covers her sight, and the scene closes.
